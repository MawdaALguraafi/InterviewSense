Frequently Asked Questions

What is InterviewSense?

InterviewSense is an advanced AI-driven platform that helps candidates prepare for technical job interviews. Our system combines interview simulations with facial emotion analysis to provide comprehensive feedback on both your verbal responses and non-verbal cues.

Is Interview Sense suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! InterviewSense is designed for candidates at all levels, from beginners to experienced professionals. Our system adapts to your skill level and provides appropriate questions and feedback to help you improve wherever you are in your career journey.

What about pricing?

InterviewSense offers flexible pricing options to suit different needs. We have free features to get you started, as well as premium subscription plans that provide access to advanced features. Visit our pricing page for detailed information on our current plans and offers.

How do I get started with InterviewSense?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account on our platform, fill in some basic information about your field and the positions you're targeting, and you'll be ready to begin your first practice interview. Our intuitive interface will guide you through the entire process.

Can I use Interview Sense without turning on my camera?

While you can use InterviewSense without a camera, we highly recommend enabling video for the full experience. Our facial emotion analysis provides valuable insights into your non-verbal communication, which is a crucial aspect of successful interviewing. However, if you prefer, you can still practice with just the verbal component.

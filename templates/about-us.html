About us

Welcome to InterviewSense

At Interview Sense, we are redefining the way you prepare for technical job interviews. Our website offers an advanced, AI-driven solution that blends interview simulations with facial emotion analysis to provide a truly immersive practice experience. We help you identify and improve both the content of your answers and the non-verbal cues that can make or break an interview.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional interview practice and the real-life experience of a high-stakes technical interview. We want to empower candidates with the tools they need to showcase their skills confidently, helping them land their dream jobs in the competitive tech industry.
